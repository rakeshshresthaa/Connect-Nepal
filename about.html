About Connect Nepal

Building bridges between cultures through student engagement in Nepal

Our Mission

Connect Nepal is dedicated to fostering cultural understanding and creating a supportive community for international students in Nepal. We believe in the power of cultural exchange and the importance of creating a home away from home for students from around the world.

Our Values

Cultural Diversity

Celebrating and embracing the rich tapestry of global cultures.

Community Support

Creating a supportive network for international students.

Innovation

Finding creative ways to connect and engage students.

Inclusivity

Ensuring everyone feels welcome and valued.
